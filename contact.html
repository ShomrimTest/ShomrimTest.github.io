    TISH is a small, independent development team founded in 2022 with a passion for seamlessly and accessibly coordinating Jewish death and mourning rituals. We're currently based in Austin, TX, but the congregations and minyanim we work with are spread out across the globe. We believe in the importance of human dignity, of demystifying death, and in staying grounded in our traditions while we look towards a changing future.

    Our Team

    Gail Tosto

    Gail reinvigorated the tradition of shmirah in Austin, TX, in 1999, and worked tirelessly and diligently as the Shmirah Coordinator for Kehillah Kedoshah Congregation Agudas Achim in Austin, TX for twenty-three years. She still serves as the Regional Shmirah Coordinator, helping communities across the Greater Austin area to work together and honor their meitim, their deceased.
